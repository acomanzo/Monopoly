HOUSE RULES

  1. When you role doubles, you role again and then move your player piece.
  2. No bidding for land; you must land on a space to buy it.
  3. There is no banker since any player can access the bank.
  4. A player may buy any studio or brownstone for any of their owned properties. i.e. you don't have to evenly build up a color group.
  5. A player landing on a property must always pay rent because player's cannot ask each other.
  6. You must pay $200 when you land on income tax. Players don't have the choice to pay 10% of their worth.
  7. Mortgage has been removed from the game. So, a player must always pay rent when they land on owned property.
  8. When a player goes bankrupt, all their assets are up for grabs for any player that lands on that property. There is no auction. It can be purchased with its studios/brownstones for its original cost.
